DriveSheet Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

DriveSheet ("the App") is a business mileage and trip tracking application developed by Timothy Le. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

DriveSheet collects minimal information necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Trip data — dates, mileage, vehicle names, and business purposes that you manually enter
  • Google account email — used solely to identify your connected Google account for Sheets sync

We do not collect location data, GPS coordinates, contact information, payment information, or any other personal data.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

  • On your device — Trip and vehicle data is stored locally on your iPhone using encrypted device storage
  • In your Google Drive — When you enable Google Sheets sync, trip data is written directly to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive account. We do not store your data on our servers.

3. Google Integration

DriveSheet uses Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with your Google account. This allows the App to read and write data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet that you own. The App requests the following Google permissions:

  • View and manage your Google Drive files (used to create and locate your mileage spreadsheet)
  • View and manage your Google Sheets spreadsheets (used to sync trip entries)

DriveSheet's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your trip data belongs to you and lives in your own Google Drive.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete the app, and in your Google Drive until you delete the spreadsheet. You have full control over your data at all times.

6. Children's Privacy

DriveSheet is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Security

Google OAuth tokens are stored in your device's secure encrypted storage. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
